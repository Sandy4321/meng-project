\section{Variance of Sample Average}
Given random variables $X_1, ..., X_n$  each with variance $\sigma^2$, $Var(\bar{X}_n) = \frac{\sigma^2}{n}$.

\begin{proof}
\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
Var(\bar{X}_n) &= Var\left(\frac{X_1, ..., X_n}{n}\right) \\
&= (\frac{1}{n})^2 \sum_{i=1}^{n} \sigma_i^2 \\
&= \frac{\sigma^2}{n} \\
\end{split}
\end{equation*}
\end{proof}
